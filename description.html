Maze Game Gameplay

Welcome to the Maze Game, where your goal is to navigate through a complex maze and reach the exit. In this game, you'll face various challenges as you attempt to find your way out of the maze.

Objective

The main objective of the game is to find the exit located somewhere within the maze. You must use your problem-solving skills to navigate through the labyrinth and avoid dead ends.

Gameplay Mechanics

- **Movement**: Use the arrow keys or WASD keys to move your character through the maze.
- **Maze Layout**: The maze is generated with walls and pathways that you must navigate through.
- **Timers and Score**: Complete the maze as quickly as possible to achieve a high score.

Features

- **Dynamic Maze Generation**: Each game features a new maze layout, offering endless replayability.
- **Difficulty Levels**: Choose from different difficulty levels that affect the size and complexity of the maze.
- **Hints and Tools**: Use in-game hints or tools to help you find the exit if you get stuck.

Controls

- **Arrow Keys / WASD**: Move your character through the maze.
- **Escape Key**: Pause the game or access the menu.
